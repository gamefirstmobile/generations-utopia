About the game

A game about building a city for every generation.

Generations: Utopia is a city-builder simulation where you manage a growing city across five generations — the Silent Generation, Boomers, Gen X, Millennials and Gen Z.

Each generation has its own values, demands and grievances. Build roads, develop the economy, and deal with protests, crises, and social tension — all while every generation is convinced you're doing it completely wrong.

More than a city-builder. A deep societal simulation where decisions have real, unpredictable consequences.

Key systems

What makes it different

01 / Transport & Logistics
Roads, Transit & Mobility

Build transport networks that every generation judges differently. Boomers want parking; Gen Z wants bike lanes. You decide.

02 / Economy & Development
Markets & Growth

Balance economic policies across conflicting generational interests. Every reform pleases one group and angers another.

03 / Infrastructure & Energy
Power & Resources

Keep the lights on, the grid stable, and the older generation from blaming you for the blackouts they predicted.

04 / Society & Culture
Culture & Social Life

Theatres, parks, festivals. What counts as culture depends entirely on who you ask — and someone is always asking.

05 / Safety & Ecology
Security & Environment

Protests, riots, natural disasters and environmental crises. React fast — or spend a decade rebuilding trust you never had.
